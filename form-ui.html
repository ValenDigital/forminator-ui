Forminator Forms

Here you can find all elements related to Forminator forms as well as its design variations.

Form elements can be placed in a maximum of 4 columns per row and the spacing for the grid comes in three variations: comfortable, compact and custom.

Comfortable spacing will add 30px distance between columns.
Compact spacing will add 14px distance between columns.
Custom will allow users to add the desired distance between columns. Give it a try:
